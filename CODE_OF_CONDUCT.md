# Code of Conduct

At [RuneWerkz], we are committed to fostering an academic community where freedom of speech is
respected as a fundamental human right that belongs to everyone, regardless of nationality or background. We believe
that open dialogue and respectful communication are essential for meaningful interactions. It is only through those
meaningful conversations, that real progress is made. Liberty or death. This Code of Conduct outlines
our standards for behavior within our community, and we ask all members to abide by these principles.

## Freedom of Speech and Responsible Communication

We value Liberty as a cornerstone of our community. Freedom of speech is the cornerstone for this. We believe firmly
that ideas must be discussed openly,and that all viewpoints have merit. Discrimination based on beliefs is tantamount to
racism in our eyes. No one should be excluded at a surface level due to their personal beliefs.

## Limits

Limits are needed to maximize freedom for all, but those limits are placed as a last resort. Our explicit limitations
are as follows:

1. Child Exploitation: Sharing explicit or sexual content involving minors (child pornography) is illegal in almost all
   jurisdictions due to its serious criminal nature. There is no second chance here. We will ban you as soon as
   possible.
2. Malware/Unlawful Surveillance: Using our platform to distribute malware of any kind is treated on the same level as
   child exploitation. We believe all users should know what data of theirs is being collected, how it's being used, and
   where it's being sent. Any attempts to subvert this are contrary to our beliefs.
3. Harassment/Threats/Doxxing: Posting personal information with the clear intent to harass, threaten, intimidate,
   blackmail, or in any way do physical harm to individuals. Repeated offenses after clear instruction to stop are how
   we define harassment. All information covered under PCI/HIPPA information also qualify.
4. Copyright/Trademarks: Profiting off an IP you don't own is illegal and must be enforced. We understand and will fight
   for instances of fair use where possible.

## Enforcement and Consequences

Breaches of this Code of Conduct will not be taken lightly. In cases of violation, appropriate actions will be taken, 
ranging from warnings to temporary or permanent bans, depending on the severity of the offense.

For severe cases that involve illegal activities, we reserve the right to report such breaches to the relevant
authorities.

## Empowering the Community

We believe in the power of community-driven moderation. We can't oversee every conversation, and frankly, do not want to 
assume the expensive and impossible responsibility. Instead, we believe in empowering the community to self moderate. 
Building tools that allow admins/moderators to effectively deal with issues as they arise.  We believe that natural selection aids
as servers/communities that do not self moderate die out.  It is on YOU the member of the community to take part in it.
Even if that's as simple as foot-traffic.
